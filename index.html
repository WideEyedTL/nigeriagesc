About N-GESC

Introduction

The Nigeria Geosciences and Engineering Students Community (N-GESC) ignited its journey in January 2023. More than just a student group, N-GESC is a vibrant ecosystem where academic knowledge meets practical fire, where potential sparks collaboration, and where responsible development takes root. Its inaugural spark was a historical exhibition and workshops held in Kogi state, a landscape rich in both resources and lessons.

Background Story

N-GESC empowers the next generation of Nigerian geoscientists and engineers to develop sustainable solutions for the nation's energy, infrastructure, and environmental challenges. We connect passionate students with industry professionals, academics, and peers through engaging workshops, conferences, field trips, and mentorship programs. N-GESC is your gateway to a thriving community, valuable resources, and a career path shaping Nigeria's future.

The towering furnaces of Dangote Cement and the intricate machinery of Ajaokuta Steel Company served as living classrooms, transforming theoretical concepts into tangible experiences. Students, hailing from institutions like PTI, FUPRE, FUTA, FUTMINNA, and Kwara State University, witnessed real-world giants in action, sparking curiosity and igniting a sense of purpose in these future leaders.

But N-GESC's impact wasn't confined to Kogi's borders. The community rapidly bloomed, attracting students from across the nation, their diverse backgrounds forming a tapestry of shared passion and ambition. Partnerships with industry giants like Federal University of Technology, Akure, and the Nigeria International Energy Summit provided vital fuel for this growth, while the establishment of student chapters in universities sowed the seeds of a nationwide network. Our workshops, conferences, and field trips became fertile ground for nurturing young minds.
