Al-Amin Saheed Babatunde

Chief Dream Monger & Founder

A distinguished geoscientist and accomplished student leader, graduated with honours from the prestigious Federal University of Technology, Akure. As the President of the Nigerian Mining and Geosciences Society, he not only maintained an exceptional academic record but also spearheaded initiatives that left a lasting impact. Al-Amin's expertise in Machine Learning is evident in his groundbreaking Flood Vulnerability Mapping projects, showcasing his commitment to addressing societal challenges.

Founder of the Nigeria Geosciences and Engineering Students Community, Al-Amin's leadership extended to fostering industry connections and practical knowledge. His notable achievements include securing exhibition space at the Nigeria International Energy Summit 2024, marking a historic milestone for the Federal University of Technology, Akure. Committed to education, he established the Nigeria Geosciences and Engineering Students Community and Bright Brains Education Centres, contributing significantly to student empowerment.

Al-Amin's global influence is demonstrated by the success of crowdfunding for his Flood Vulnerability Mapping projects in Nigeria, where collaboration with international mentors, played a pivotal role. Aspiring to be a global leader in sustainable development, he seeks to leverage his skills for the benefit of future geoscientists and engineers, reflecting his dedication to positive change on a global scale.
